WELCOME TO THE END OF THE WORLD
Find a way out of the Bunker>
-

It is the year 2025 and walkers have taken control of the land,
While out on a supply run, you have come across a bunker filled with food and supplies.
Being caughtup with the plunder, you failed to see the boobie trapped door slam shut behind you.
There is a lock on the door and in order to escape you must break the code with the clues around you.
Be careful, if you enter the wrong letter into the lock another zombie will be released into the bunker.
With no ammo or weapons, time isnt on your side. Can you make it out alive?
